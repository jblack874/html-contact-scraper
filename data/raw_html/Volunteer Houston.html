WHAT WE DO

Volunteer Houston, a program of IM Houston, connects people with meaningful opportunities to serve across Greater Houston. We bring together volunteers, agencies, and companies to strengthen Houston’s culture of service and build a more compassionate city.

As a proud Points of Light Global Affiliate, we are part of the world’s largest network dedicated to volunteerism. Through our online portal and community partnerships, we make it easy to give back, respond in times of disaster, and help create a stronger, more resilient Houston.

Your Volunteer Houston Roadmap

From curiosity to community impact, here’s how your journey with Volunteer Houston can unfold.

1

Discover

Learn about local needs and explore volunteer opportunities in Houston that spark your interest.

2

Connect

Create your volunteer profile and connect with nonprofits across our Greater Houston service area.

3

Serve

Show up, volunteer, and support the causes and communities that matter most to you.

4

Grow & Share

Reflect on your experience, share your story, and keep finding new ways to make Houston stronger.

Every step you take adds up to a more connected, compassionate Houston.
